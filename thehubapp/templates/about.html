About Us

The Hub Terminus Limited was started to enable Micro Small & Medium Enterprises, independent Business owners, as well as other Brands grow sales, profit and fulfilment proficiency. Coming from years of Managing Transportation, Product Distribution and Fulfilment Services within the Logistics Value Chain, at executive level, we aim to provide seamless Order Fulfilment Solution with low cost and Best-In-Class Service. This is aimed at enhancing the scalability rate of SMEs Pan-Nigeria and globally. We seek to disrupt the logistics space with nimble cloud-based technology that will help our partners rapidly grow sales, optimize operations with ease.

We help e-commerce vendors and Direct To Customer (D2C) brand with fulfilment, while they focus on market expansion. We Receive your stock, store, fulfil and distribute Pan-Nigeria and Internationally. Our 3PL services are bespoke to your business needs, as we understand that every eCommerce business is unique from product Preparation - Delivery; Pay-As-You-Go Storage, Pick, Pack, Label, Delivery and Returns Management.

Our reliable Fulfilment Services and connected End-End Technology, which powers our fulfilment network, we aim to simplify storage, shorten transit times, cut shipping cost and above all, offer top notch delivery experience to your customers.

Our Fulfilment centre is strategically placed in the heart of Lagos mainland, within close proximity to Distribution Hubs, Vendors, Sea, Air and Land Ports. We are specifically designed for high value, fast-moving consumer goods.
